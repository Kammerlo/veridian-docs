# Change Passcode

The passcode can be modified at any time as needed.
To do so, navigate to the menu tab on the home screen and click the gear icon in the top right corner to access the settings menu and select the “Change Passcode” option.

<div className="galery-container">
    <Image src="/img/common/menu-tab.png" width={300} height={300} />

    <Image src="/img/common/settings-menu.png" width={300} height={300} />

</div>

Enter the current passcode to verify your identity.
Then, enter the new passcode twice to ensure it has been typed correctly.
