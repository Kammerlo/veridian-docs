# Creating and signing a ballot

After you have logged in Cardano Ballot, it's time to cast your votes in Cardano Ballot.

#### Nominee Selection

From the landing page, click on the “Start Voting” button to go to the categories page.

On the categories page, select the category and nominee that you want to vote for and then click on the “Vote Now” button.

#### Vote casting

In the Identity Wallet, a new page will appear prompting you to sign the vote information using your identifier. Click on the “Sign” button to finalise and submit your vote.

Once your vote has been processed, you can check the receipt by clicking on the “View Receipt” button.

Congratulations! You have successfully cast your vote in Cardano Ballot using the Identity Wallet.
