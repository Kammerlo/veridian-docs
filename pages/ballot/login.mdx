# Login and Vote Receipts

To check vote receipts in Cardano Ballot, it is necessary to log in to confirm your identity before accessing the receipts.

#### Login

In the top left corner of the Cardano Ballot window, the wallet button should be clicked to expand the drop-down menu, and the "Login" option should be selected.

A new page will appear in the Identity Wallet, prompting the signing of login information with your identifier. The “Sign” button should be clicked to confirm your identity.

#### Vote Receipts

Once logged in to Cardano Ballot, the vote receipt can be checked by clicking on the “View Receipt” button.
